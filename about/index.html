About

Hi, I'm Thomas.
A photographer from Norway.

I have an interest in places that still retain their traditional features amidst the stronger forces of our age. And I like photographing the people in them.

The world is a place of beauty, wonder and mystery, and of injustice and suffering, told and untold. I aspire towards capturing those moments I deem meaningful in the facets of life, both in their ugly and beautiful shape.
